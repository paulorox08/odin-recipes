Boiled Eggs

Description

Perfectly boiled eggs cooked to your liking!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Pour cold water into a pot
  2. Once simmering, place eggs gently into pot
  3. Start timer
  4. For soft boiled, simmer for 4 minutes
  5. For medium, simmer for 5 minutes
  6. For hard, simmer for 8 minutes
