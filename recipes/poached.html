Poached Eggs

Description

Perfectly poached eggs every time!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Once boiling, create a whirlpool using a spoon
  3. Drop eggs into the whirlpool
  4. Depending on how you like your eggs, two minutes is enough for the yolk to be runny
  5. Remove eggs and sprinkle with salt and pepper
