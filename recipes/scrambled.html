Scrambled Eggs

Description

A simple and delicious recipe to make the perfect scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pan and add butter
  2. Beat the eggs
  3. Pour eggs into pan and constantly fold the edges into the middle
  4. Once cooked to your liking, remove and plate
  5. Sprinke salt and pepper onto egg
