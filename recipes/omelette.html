Omelette

Description

A simple and delicious omelette recipe that takes no time to make and is very budget friendly.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Herb of choice

Steps

  1. Heat the pan and add butter
  2. Beat the eggs
  3. Pour eggs into pan
  4. Put cheese and tomatoes on one side of egg
  5. Fold egg over filling
  6. Chop herbs
  7. Plate omelette and garnish with herbs
